2. Description of Service

The AirBagWeight App connects to Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) pressure sensors installed on air suspension systems to provide estimated axle weight readings for trucks and trailers. The App allows users to configure truck and trailer profiles, assign sensors to axles, view estimated weight data, record weight history, and export weight records.

3. Weight Accuracy Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: PLEASE READ THIS SECTION CAREFULLY.

The AirBagWeight App provides estimated weight readings only. These estimates are derived from air pressure sensor data and user-configured calibration values. The weight figures displayed by the App are not certified measurements and should never be relied upon as a substitute for weighing your vehicle on a certified scale.
